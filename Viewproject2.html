Project Online School

Designed for a contemporary e-learning platform, this landing page blends bold visuals with a youthful color scheme to engage students and parents alike. Featuring vibrant cutout portraits of diverse learners, the design exudes inclusivity and modern education values. The layout is split with one side focused on messaging and CTAs (Start Today, Registration), while the other dynamically showcases user personas. Navigation is straightforward, categorized into school levels (Elementary, Middle, High), allowing easy discovery. The dark gradient background contrasts beautifully with playful shapes and pops of color, making it ideal for progressive EdTech startups.
